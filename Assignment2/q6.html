Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta in olive oil until crispy, then add the garlic.
  3. Whisk the eggs and mix with parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pancetta mixture.
  5. Pour the egg mixture over the hot pasta, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately, topped with extra parmesan cheese if desired.

Dish Image

Fun Facts

Did you know? Spaghetti Carbonara is believed to have originated in Rome, Italy, and was inspired by American soldiers during World War II.

Fun fact: The key to a perfect carbonara is making sure the eggs don't scramble. Stir quickly to make a smooth, creamy sauce!
